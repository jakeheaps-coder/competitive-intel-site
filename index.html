How It Works

The Daily Intelligence Pipeline

Every morning at 7 AM MST, a Cloud Run job kicks off the full competitive research cycle — from live web search to structured storage to stakeholder delivery.

01 Load Competitor Dataset

Queries the competitors_to_watch dataset from Domo via Code Engine — 18 unique competitors mapped across 5 product pillars (Visualization, Integration, Agent Catalyst, Apps, Embed).

Domo Code EngineREST APISQL Query

02 Research Each Competitor

For each competitor, runs 3 Gemini searches with Google Search grounding: recent news & product launches, head-to-head Domo vs. comparison, and live pricing/features page. Executes in parallel with 4 workers.

Gemini 2.5 FlashGoogle Search54 Queries4 Workers

03 Track Misconceptions & M&A Signals

Runs 9 targeted queries to find false/outdated Domo claims on Reddit, G2, and AI chatbots. Plus 30 M&A queries scanning Bloomberg, Reuters, WSJ, Seeking Alpha, StockTwits, Reddit, and X for acquisition signals.

9 Misconception Queries30 M&A QueriesDeduplication

04 Store, Score & Deliver

Writes ~100 structured documents to Domo AppDB (per-competitor collections). Every finding gets a sentiment score (threat/opportunity/neutral) and confidence level. Generates an HTML email digest grouped by pillar and delivers to stakeholders.

20 AppDB CollectionsHTML Email DigestSentiment Scoring

Systematically finds and corrects false or outdated claims about Domo — then validates the truth via the Knowledge Graph API.

  • False claims from Reddit, G2, AI chatbots & blogs
